Travelling Destinations

Goa

Goa, located on the western coast of India, is a popular tourist destination known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. It offers a mix of serene natural beauty, historical churches, and lively beach shacks. Famous spots include Baga Beach, Dudhsagar Falls, and Fort Aguada. Goa is also known for its seafood, flea markets, and music festivals.

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, also known as the "Palace of Winds," is a stunning pink sandstone palace located in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh, it features 953 small windows (jharokhas) designed to allow royal women to observe street festivals without being seen. Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the most iconic monuments in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is a symbol of eternal love. Constructed from white marble, the monument features intricate carvings, beautiful gardens, and a majestic dome. Completed in 1653, it attracts millions of visitors every year and is considered one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a sacred Sikh shrine located in Amritsar, Punjab. It is famous for its stunning golden architecture, spiritual ambiance, and the holy Amrit Sarovar (pool) surrounding it. Built by Guru Arjan Dev in the 16th century, the temple welcomes people of all faiths. The complex also houses the world's largest free community kitchen (Langar), serving meals to thousands daily. It is a symbol of peace, equality, and devotion.
