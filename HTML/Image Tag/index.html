IPL franchises

1.Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians (MI) is a franchise cricket team in the Indian Premier League (IPL), owned by Reliance Industries. Established in 2008, MI is one of the most successful teams in IPL history, having won the title five times (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020). The team is known for its strong core, featuring players like Rohit Sharma, Jasprit Bumrah, and Hardik Pandya. MI plays its home matches at the iconic Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai.

2.Chennai Super Kings


Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a prominent IPL franchise based in Chennai, owned by India Cements. Led by the legendary MS Dhoni, CSK is known for its consistency and strong fan base. The team has won the IPL title five times (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, and 2023). Their home ground is the MA Chidambaram Stadium, also known as Chepauk.

3.Sunrisers Hydrabad

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is an IPL franchise based in Hyderabad, owned by Sun TV Network. The team made its debut in 2013 and won its maiden IPL title in 2016 under the captaincy of David Warner. Known for its strong bowling attack, SRH has featured top players like Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Rashid Khan. Their home ground is the Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium in Hyderabad.

4.Royal Challengers Bangaluru

Royal Challengers Bangaluru (RCB) is an IPL franchise based in Bengaluru, owned by United Spirits. Despite having a massive fan base and star players like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Faf du Plessis, RCB is yet to win an IPL title. The team is known for its explosive batting line-up and passionate supporters. Their home ground is the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru.

5.Gujrat Titans

Gujarat Titans are a professional IPL team based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. They made a stunning debut in 2022 by winning the IPL title under Hardik Pandya’s captaincy. The team is currently led by Shubman Gill and coached by Ashish Nehra. Their home ground is the iconic Narendra Modi Stadium.

6.Kolkata Knight Riders

Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are a popular IPL team based in Kolkata, West Bengal. They have won the IPL title twice, in 2012 and 2014, under Gautam Gambhir's captaincy. The team is co-owned by Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan and plays its home matches at Eden Gardens. KKR is known for its passionate fanbase and competitive spirit.

7.Punjab Kings

Punjab Kings (PBKS) are an IPL franchise based in Mohali, Punjab. Originally known as Kings XI Punjab, the team was renamed in 2021. They play their home matches at the PCA Stadium in Mohali. Despite having strong players over the years, PBKS is yet to win an IPL title but remains a fan-favorite for its fighting spirit.

8.Delhi Capitals

Delhi Capitals (DC) are an IPL team based in Delhi, originally known as Delhi Daredevils. The team was rebranded in 2018 and has shown significant improvement since then. Their home ground is the Arun Jaitley Stadium in Delhi. Although they haven’t won an IPL title yet, they reached the finals in 2020 and continue to be strong contenders.

9.Lucknow Super Giants

Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) are one of the newest franchises in the IPL, based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. They made their debut in the 2022 season and quickly became a competitive team. LSG plays its home matches at the Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium. The team is known for its balanced squad and strong performances in the league stages.

10.Rajasthan Royals

Rajasthan Royals (RR) are an IPL team based in Jaipur, Rajasthan. They were the inaugural IPL champions in 2008, led by Shane Warne. Known for nurturing young talent, RR has produced several cricketing stars over the years. Despite some ups and downs, the team remains a strong contender and plays its home matches at the Sawai Mansingh Stadium in Jaipur.
